About SuiteLife


SuiteLife is here to provide a platform for communal living. As population growth outpaces housing availability, SuiteLife believes a greater percentage of the population will find themselves in shared housing. We know sharing space might be easy, but keeping track of all the unique aspects of living with others isn’t! Inspired by campus residential life at Harvey Mudd College, the SuiteLife team has put together both an iOS and a web application to be platforms that encourage communication among members of shared housing.
